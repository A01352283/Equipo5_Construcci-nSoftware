Welcome to Percussion Islands!

Greetings adventurer! You have come across unique islands where the habitants are passionate about percussive instruments. As you begin your journey, you will face various obstacles that will block your way. At this point, you will need to take on multiple minigames that will put your knowledge of percussion instruments to the test. By playing these games and reading various books from the houses, you will unlock instruments that will let you continue your journey.


This game seeks to introduce the vast world of percussion instruments with different minigames to those who don't have a strong background in music. Exploring the game islands and learning some of the main techniques and recommendations in percussion instruments (either from the different books or minigames) will allow for a better learning experience.
