First conceived as a game proposal for the Percussive Arts Society (PAS), Percussion Island quickly became a vision of creating a video game that would generate genuine interest of the player in learning more about percussion instruments. We intend to provoke a Mindset of awe and joy for the player during gameplay.


It is necessary to address that during the game development process, we received guidance not only from our teachers and classmates but also from musicians from the organization as well. These interactions allowed us to understand some of the visions PAS had in mind for the video game, such as teaching technical aspects of how percussion instruments are played and taken care of.


At the end of the day, we consider that our game has the potential to show the importance of percussion in music, both in fun and educational ways.
